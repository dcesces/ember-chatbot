The Story of Ember

When people encounter injuries or emergencies, they often turn to search engines for help. But the information found online can be overwhelming, confusing, and even contradictory.

Ember was created to solve this problem—a reliable, comforting safety companion that provides clear, accurate first-aid guidance during those critical moments.

Ember exists to simplify safety. Together, let's make emergencies less stressful and lives safer. 💡
